Dosa

Description

Follow this recipe to create the world's best dosa! Make sure to have an appetite!

Ingredients

  • rice
  • urad dal
  • fenugreek seeds
  • salt
  1. Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for 4-6 hours, then grind them together into a smooth batter.
  2. Ferment the batter by keeping it in a warm place for 8-12 hours until it rises and becomes slightly sour.
  3. Add salt to the fermented batter, then spread a thin layer on a hot greased griddle in a circular motion
  4. Cook until the edges turn golden brown, add a little oil around the edges, and flip briefly if desired before serving.
