Lasagna

Description

Follow this recipe to create the world's best lasagna! Make sure to have an appetite!

Ingredients

  • tomato sauce
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • garlic powder
  1. Make a meat sauce with ground beef, onions, garlic, tomatoes, and herbs.
  2. Prepare the ricotta mixture by combining ricotta cheese, eggs, and Parmesan.
  3. Assemble in layers: sauce on the bottom of the baking dish, then pasta sheets, cheese mixture, more sauce, and repeat until you reach the top. Finish with sauce and a generous sprinkle of mozzarella.
  4. Bake covered at 375Â°F for 25 minutes, then uncovered for another 15 minutes until bubbly and golden.
