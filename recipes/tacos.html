Tacos

Description

Follow this recipe to create the world's best tacos! Make sure to have an appetite!

Ingredients

  • Tortillas (corn or flour)
  • Seasoned meat (beef, chicken, or pork)
  • Shredded lettuce or cabbage
  • Cheese (cotija, cheddar, or monterey jack)
  1. Cook and season your protein (ground beef, chicken, etc.) with taco seasoning.
  2. Warm your tortillas in a skillet or microwave until soft and pliable.
  3. Assemble by placing meat in the tortilla, then adding toppings like cheese, lettuce, and salsa.
  4. Fold or roll the tortilla and enjoy immediately.
